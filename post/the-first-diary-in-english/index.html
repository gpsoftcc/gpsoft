The first diary in English

2019-09-20

I think I should write a diary in English every day because it can help me to improve my English writing skills. In the beginning, there will be a lot of mistakes. But I believe that my English writing skills will be improved if I insist on do it.
Now, I understand one thing through many years of exam--- If your mother language writing skills are not really good, maybe your English writing skills are not good too, because if you don't know how to write a good article in your native language, you also don't know how to write a good article in English. I think it is easy to understand.
In recent days, I study English most of the time and I always feel time flies. It is a really good thing. Because if you feel time flies you must like the things which you are doing now.
Wow, although this diary is written very terrible, it cost me a lot of time. It is time to go to work.
